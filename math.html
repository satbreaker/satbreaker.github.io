Step One: Read the Question Carefully

The most important part of the SAT Math section is ironically reading. Most kids who take the test understand how to use the math that the question requires, but by not fully reading the questions, kids answer questions incompletely or find answers to questions other than the one asked. Don't fall into the trap of unneccesary wrong answers and read the whole question!

Step Two: Answer the Question

Use the formulas and mathematical skills you have learned to answer the question. Use the space given for work and never do mental math; always write everything down so you'll be more likely to find mistakes that you make! In addition, don't forget to use your calculator for everything!

Step Three: Most Importantly, Review!

Like all of the other sections, make sure you check your work! Look at your notes to make sure they are no silly mistakes, and make sure you answer the question they are asking. For example, don't give the answer how many oranges you ate yesterday when they are looking for how many apples that you ate today!
